What Nexus does

Built for how you actually work

Stop context-switching. Nexus ingests your tools and becomes the connective layer your team has been missing.

Context-aware AI

Nexus reads your open tabs, tickets, and docs to understand what you're working on — then proactively surfaces relevant context without being asked.

Universal integrations

Connect GitHub, Linear, Slack, Notion, Jira, Figma and 60+ more. Nexus becomes a single pane of glass across your entire stack in minutes.

Smart prioritization

Let Nexus analyze dependencies and deadlines across projects, then rank your daily work list so the most impactful tasks are always on top.

Intelligent alerts

No more notification floods. Nexus batches, deduplicates, and scores incoming signals so you only see what actually needs your attention — when it needs it.

Team memory

Nexus builds a shared knowledge graph of past decisions, RFCs, and context — so new teammates onboard fast and institutional knowledge never walks out the door.

Enterprise-grade privacy

SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Zero data training on your content. Private cloud deployments available.
