Common questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens when my free trial ends?

We'll send a reminder 3 days before your trial ends. If you don't add a payment method, your workspace automatically moves to the Free plan — no data is lost.

Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?

We do. Eligible early-stage startups and registered non-profits can apply for 50% off any paid plan — reach out to our team with proof of eligibility.
